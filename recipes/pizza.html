Pizza

Description

Pizza is a golden circular dough disk from heaven. Greasy and sliced into triangles, nothing else compares to the illustious reputation of this delicacy.


Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Desired toppings

Instructions

  1. Coat dough in tomato sauce
  2. Coat sauce in cheese
  3. Add desired toppings
  4. Bake for 26 minutes at 350F
