Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wonderful classic. Essentially acting as a casserole of noodles, this zesty Italian classic has been a staple in many homes for generations.


Often prepared with a mix of tomato sauce, herbs, cheese and meat, there are also many options for those with more restrictive diets. Vegetarian lasagna is a good alternative for those looking to avoid meat while still enjoying dairy. Vegan lasagna, on the other hand, foregoes all animal-based products in favor of a fully plant-based meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Prep noodles and sauce in pan, layering sauce-noodles-sauce
  2. Add spices
  3. Add cheese
  4. Bake for 2 hours at 350F
