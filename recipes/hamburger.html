Hambuger

Description

A hamburger is an American favorite. Not much beats a slab of greasy hamburger meat on bread, paired with all the toppings one might desire to slot between meat and bread.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Cheese
  • Buns
  • Ketchup
  • Anything more than this list is heresy!

Instructions

  1. Pad hamberger meat into a patty
  2. Cook on iron skillet; flip occasionally
  3. Put WELL DONE patty on bread
  4. Add cheese and ketchup; enjoy! :)
